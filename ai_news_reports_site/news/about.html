About TL;DR News

TL;DR News is an AI-powered news intelligence platform that delivers concise, automated summaries of the day's most important stories across five categories, updated three times daily.

What We Do

Every day at 7 AM, 4 PM, and 8 PM MST, our automated pipeline fetches the latest headlines from trusted news sources, analyzes sentiment, identifies key themes, and generates structured intelligence reports using AI. The result is a daily briefing that helps you stay informed without information overload.

Our Sources

We aggregate news from established outlets across five categories:

  • World — BBC News
  • Business — The Guardian
  • Technology — The Verge
  • Sports — ESPN
  • Science — ScienceDaily

How It Works

Our pipeline is fully automated with no human editing. Each report goes through several stages: RSS feed collection, headline deduplication, sentiment analysis using the VADER lexicon, AI-powered report generation with key themes and future outlook, and finally publication as both interactive reports and static HTML pages.

What Each Report Includes

  • Executive summary of the day's news
  • Key themes identified across headlines
  • Notable headlines with analysis of why they matter
  • Sentiment analysis (positive, negative, neutral, or mixed)
  • Future outlook with short-term and medium-term projections
  • A daily roundup article synthesizing stories across all categories

Transparency

All reports are AI-generated and clearly labeled as such. We include confidence levels and caveats with every report. Our sentiment scores are computed algorithmically. We do not editorialize or inject opinion — our goal is to summarize and analyze, not to persuade.

Original source links are provided for every headline so you can always verify the underlying reporting.
